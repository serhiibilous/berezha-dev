Vlad Styran

Co-founder & VP, Business Development

Vlad Styran is an internationally known cybersecurity professional and leader with 15+ years of experience within Penetration Testing, Social Engineering, and Security Awareness.

He currently holds OSCP, CISSP, and CISA credentials and was certified as C|EH, ISO27001LA, and many more throughout his career.

To help companies better protect their critical data and staff from modern cyber threats, he has created a unique methodology of identifying, assessing, and treating "human factor" security issues.

Vlad is one of the OWASP Kyiv chapter founders and a co-founder and organizer at NoNameCon, a practical cybersecurity conference. He is a notable blogger, podcast producer, and conference speaker.

At Berezha Security, Vlad is responsible for business growth and customer experience. His involvement allows us to deliver first-rate cybersecurity services and consult clients on a broad range of cybersecurity matters: software security, cybersecurity awareness, cybersecurity strategy, and security investment.

Using our offensive mindset and experience, we help our customers develop a mindful approach to cybersecurity and integrate security principles into all aspects of their business.
