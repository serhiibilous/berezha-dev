Kostiantyn Korsun
Co-founder & COO

As former deputy head of Cybercrime Division at Security Service of Ukraine, Kostyantyn is a co-founder and the first head of CERT-UA. After resigning, Kos acted as Regional Director for the Ukrainian Research Office at iSIGHT Partners, an international cyber threat intelligence firm later acquired by FireEye Inc.

He is a prominent Ukrainian cybersecurity community member, a co-founder and the first Chairman of the Ukrainian Information Security Group (UISG), and a long-term head organizer of the UISGCON professional conference.

In BSG, he acts as an Executive Director and expert coach for the team, who shares his vast experience in cybersecurity, cybercrime research, and threat analysis.
